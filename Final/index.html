JOURNEY


Sistar was a South Korean girl group formed by Starship Entertainment. The group consists of Bora, Hyolyn, Soyou, and Dasom. The group debuted in 2010 with the song Push Push. During the middle of their 'Let's Start Sharing Concert' performance, Bora suddenly fell on stage, fracturing her thumb. After the injury was dealt with, the members all returned back on stage to finish their performance. A fan taken video of this performance went viral, and they received a lot of media attention. This jump started their career, leading to many hit songs. Their image as a group was different from many others and because all of their songs were summer hits, they became known as 'Queens of Summer'. It isn't summer until Sistar comes back. Unfortuantely, as 7 years come to a close, they sent us off with their last song, 'Lonely'. While it is softer and sadder compared to their usual, upbeat songs, this video takes place at the end of a summer night, marking an end to a journey. While it was a sad goodbye, their disbandment was the most ideal compared to many others, and it gave fans hope that we will see them together again.

HIDDEN


Dasom always had rumors about her bullying the rest of the group and being casted out. That's the perception. In the end, we get to see the reality. Even when the rumors knocked her down, Sistar always defended her and helped her get right back up.
Bora was always seen hanging out with people from other groups. The perception was that she was also an outcast and that the other members didn't want to hang out with her. But in reality, the girls were always behind her. They never left her side.
While Hyolyn and Soyou's solo careers took off, the perception was that the other two members were left behind. In reality, they never fell out of the picture.
This can also be said for their subunit Sistar19. The point of them showing all of this was that it didn't matter what anyone else said, they knew the truth. They knew how strong their friendship was.
No matter what each member did individually, each summer, they came together to give us something special. And in the end, they put down their heels, signifying the end of their career.
